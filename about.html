About Velira

Who is behind Velira

Velira was created by a small collective of women in their 30s and 40s who spent years trying to understand what was happening in their own bodies — not in theory, but in practice. This experience was shaped by disrupted sleep, volatile food cravings, mood changes and heavy symptoms around PMS, and in some cases severe experiences like PMDD.

What stood out was not just the symptoms themselves, but how long it took to make sense of them and find a balance to manage them in a harmonious and practical way in our day-to-day lives.

Why we started Velira

Most of us moved through different doctors, different opinions, and advice that often felt generalized rather than truly individual. Our symptoms were often minimized or explained away as "normal," without offering a holistic and individualized path toward improvement and balance.

At the same time, the information available online rarely translated into real understanding. It was fragmented, overly simplified, or designed to capture attention and likes rather than provide clarity. Velira was built for you from that gap: not a lack of information, but a lack of coherence.

What Velira is here to do

Velira is here to help you make your hormones work for you (not against you) in every aspect of your life. It does this by giving you a toolkit to balance your mind, body, and heart with your hormonal cycle.

The intention is not to give you something to follow, but to help you recognize patterns and work with them, rather than against them.

What this means in practice

Over time, parts of Velira will become available as structured, paid offerings. At the same time, a core part of what we build will remain accessible — including insights, tools, and resources that help you get oriented and move faster.

This also helps you bring more clarity into conversations with your healthcare provider if needed. We believe that understanding your hormonal cycle should not take ten-plus years of trial and error.
