About Velira

Velira was created by a small collective of women in their 30s and 40s who spent years trying to understand what was happening in their own bodies — not in theory, but in practice. What stood out was not just the symptoms themselves, but how long it took to make sense of them.

Why we started Velira

Most of us moved through different doctors, different opinions, and advice that often felt generalized rather than truly individual. Our symptoms were often minimized or explained away as "normal," without offering a holistic path toward improvement.

Velira was built from that gap: not a lack of information, but a lack of coherence.

What Velira is here to do

Velira is here to help you make your hormones work for you — not against you — in every aspect of your life. The intention is not to give you something to follow, but to help you recognise patterns and work with them, rather than against them.

What this means in practice

Over time, parts of Velira will become available as structured, paid offerings. A core part of what we build will remain accessible. We believe that understanding your hormonal cycle should not take ten-plus years of trial and error.

Joining the Velira community

Everything we share is intended to add clarity — not override your judgment. While we aim to provide accurate and meaningful insights, we cannot guarantee outcomes.
